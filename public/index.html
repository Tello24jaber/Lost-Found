About GJU Campus Lost & Found

Help us build a supportive campus community by connecting lost items with their owners.

How It Works

Our platform helps connect GJU students and staff who have lost items with those who have found them. The process is simple:

For those who lost an item:

1. Add your lost item with a detailed description

2. Check regularly for updates on your item

3. Once found, arrange to pick up your item

For those who found an item:

1. Add the found item with a detailed description

2. Wait for the owner to contact you

3. Arrange to return the item to its owner
